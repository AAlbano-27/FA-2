Persian Cats

This is a PERSIAN CAT

  • also known as the Persian longhair, is a long-haired breed of cat characterized by a round face and short muzzle.
  • the Persian cat is the most popular pedigreed cat breed in the U.S..
  • a medium- to large-sized breed
  • coat comes in almost every color and pattern imaginable, including solid shades, silver and golden, smoke and shaded colors, tabby patterns, particolors and bicolors, and pointed colors (Himalayan).
