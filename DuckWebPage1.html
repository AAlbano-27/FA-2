Ducks

This is a DUCK

  • a small type of swimming bird with a short neck and legs
  • They have webbed feet to help them swim and broad and flat bills, which they use to feed.
  • They can live in freshwater or seawater and their feathers are waterproof! Male and female ducks often have very different appearances
  • They fly
